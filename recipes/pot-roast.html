Pot Roast

Description

The gift that keeps on giving from the slow cooker.

Here's a standard homemade delicious pot roast recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Soup Mix
  • Water
  • Vegetables: Carrots, potatoes, onions, & celery

Steps

  1. Season and sear the chuck roast.
  2. Place the ingredients in the slow cooker.
  3. Cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours.
