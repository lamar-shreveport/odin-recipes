Beijing Style Noodles

Description

Greasy, but light, and undeniably delicious.

The hardest true Chinese staple to find at an American Chinese restuarant

Learn how to make them yourself!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chicken or Beef
  • Garlic
  • Vegetables: Onion, Cucumber, Cabbage, Broccoli
  • Stir Fry Sauce
  • Water
  • Spice & Season

Steps

  1. Prepare meat and heat oil
  2. Cook the noodles and vegetables
  3. Remove from heat and add leafy greens
  4. Return to heat 1 minute
  5. Serve immediately
