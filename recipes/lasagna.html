Lasagna

Description

A rock-solid gold standard for lasagna lovers.

This one of the web's most popular lasagna recipes.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spice & Season
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
